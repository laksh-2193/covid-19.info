Precautions

"Stay at home"

Wash Hands

Wash your hands frequently with soap or detergent for atleast 20 seconds. Make use of sanitizers.

Use Masks

Always make us of Double Mask. Even if you are at home. Keep your nose and mouth covered.

Social Distancing

Keep atleast 2m distance from each other. Avoid crowded places. Stay indoors until emergency.

Medical Help

Seek medical help in case of appearance of any symptoms. Dont use any medicines without prescription.

Immunity

Do the activties that boosts your immunity like Yoga and meditation. Avoid unhealthy food

Vaccination

Get vaccinated at your chance. This will reduce your chances of getting infected. Stay away from rumours. Vaccine is 100% safe.
